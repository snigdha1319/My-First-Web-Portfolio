Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about DEVCONF 2026? We've got answers.

Is there a virtual attendance option?

No, DEVCONF 2026 is an exclusively in-person event focused on hands-on networking, live workshops, and face-to-face collaboration that cannot be replicated online.

Can I refund or transfer my ticket?

Tickets are non-refundable, but you can transfer your ticket to another person free of charge up to 7 days before the event starts (August 8, 2026).

Are meals included in the ticket price?

Yes! All ticket tiers include a networking lunch, morning coffee/tea, and afternoon snacks for all three days of the conference.

Will session recordings be available?

Yes. Standard ticket holders get 30 days of access, while Pro and Team ticket holders get lifetime access to all recorded keynotes and technical talks.
